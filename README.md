# 🌍 ClearFund

ClearFund is a Regenerative Finance (ReFi) platform designed to bridge the gap between grassroots impact projects and global supporters. We empower builders to share their vision for a better world—from climate restoration to social impact—and enable supporters to provide direct, transparent financial support through blockchain-based tipping.

## 🚀 Our Mission

We believe in a world where impact is valued and rewarded. ClearFund provides a decentralized registry for verified community projects, ensuring that every tip goes directly to the creators on the ground, with zero fees and full cryptographic transparency.

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Impact Project Discovery**: A curated and community-driven feed of ReFi projects across Climate, Education, and Social Impact labels.
- **Direct Tipping (UBI+)**: Integration with GoodDollar and Celo ecosystem to provide direct financial support to project wallets.
- **Blockchain Registry**: All projects are submitted to the `ClearFundRegistry` smart contract, creating a permanent record of impact.
- **Social & Wallet Auth**: Seamless onboarding via Privy.
- **Transparent History**: Real-time on-chain tracking of all tips and project milestones.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

### Frontend & Core
- **Framework**: [Next.js 15](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router & Server Actions)
- **Language**: JavaScript (ES6+)
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) & Vanilla CSS for premium custom designs
- **Icons**: [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/) & [React Icons](https://react-icons.github.io/react-icons/)

### Web3 & Blockchain
- **Network**: [Celo](https://celo.org/) (Mainnet & Sepolia)
- **Smart Contracts**: [Solidity](https://soliditylang.org/) (ClearFundRegistry)
- **Web3 Libraries**: [Wagmi](https://wagmi.sh/), [Viem](https://viem.sh/), [RainbowKit](https://www.rainbowkit.com/)
- **Authentication**: [Privy](https://www.privy.io/) (Social Login + Embedded Wallets)

### Backend & Storage
- **Database**: [Supabase](https://supabase.com/) (PostgreSQL)
- **ORM**: [Prisma](https://www.prisma.io/)
- **File Storage**: [IPFS](https://ipfs.io/) via [Pinata](https://www.pinata.cloud/) (Project banners, logos, and metadata)
